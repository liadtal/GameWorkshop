# Quick

A top-down grid-puzzle game where you guide a small white hamster through increasingly complex levels. Once the hamster starts moving, it **never stops** — you can only change its direction. Collect colored keys, unlock matching doors, and reach the exit alive.

**Engine:** Godot 4.x | **Genre:** Grid puzzle / action-puzzle

## How to Play

- Arrow keys / WASD to set direction
- The hamster moves continuously — plan your path carefully
- Collect keys to unlock color-matching doors
- Reach the exit to complete the level
- Hit a wall = instant death
